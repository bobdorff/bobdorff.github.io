Who does this guy think he is?

Bob Dorff is currently a student at Dev Bootcamp in Chicago. He is learning how to make the cool stuff that will define the future. In the past, Bob wrote about video games and music for a variety of defunct websites, most notably Bon Mots and Blood and Advanced Review. If you want to debate the way certain games feel under your thumbs, discuss particular types of guitar distortion or harness Bob's nascent web development skills, send an email or hit up his LinkedIn.
